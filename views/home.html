Elon Reeve Musk FRS (June 28, 1971) is an entrepreneur[2] and South African-Canadian philanthropist, naturalized American. He is the founder, chief executive officer and technical director of SpaceX; CEO of Tesla, Inc.; vice president of OpenAI, founder and CEO of Neuralink; co-founder and president of SolarCity.

As of January 7, 2021, with an estimated personal net worth of around 188, 5 billion dollars, became the richest person in the world, according to Bloomberg, surpassing entrepreneur Jeff Bezos. In the Forbes ranking, Musk occupies in 2022 in the first place.
